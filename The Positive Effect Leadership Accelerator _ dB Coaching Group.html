Frequently Asked

Can I realistically fit this into my schedule?
+
Yes — if you're intentional about it. The self-paced modules run 45–60 minutes. The live coaching session is 60 minutes, held the last Thursday of each month at 12 PM EST. Plan for about 2–3 hours per week total.
Is this for new managers or experienced leaders?
+
Both. The cohort is for anyone who manages people and wants to do it better — whether you've been leading for six months or sixteen years. The peer cohort structure means you learn from the full range of experience in the room.
What if I miss a session?
+
Modules are self-paced and available anytime. Live sessions are recorded. Missing one won't knock you out of the cohort — but the live calls are where the real work happens, so we encourage you to protect that time.
Why a cohort and not a self-paced course?
+
Because accountability changes everything. A cohort creates real relationships, real conversations, and real follow-through. You're not learning alone — you're learning with a group of people who hold each other to the work. That's what makes it stick.
